Teriyaki Ground Chicken

Description

The secret to this dish is making your own teriyaki sauce. All you need is sugar, mirin, soy sauce, and sake.

If you don't have mirin or sake, you should get them. Otherwise, buy a bottle of teriyaki sauce. But then this won't be much of a recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground chicken
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Half an onion
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp sake
  • 2 tbsp mirin
  • 1 tbsp sugar

Directions

  1. Heat the oil in a saucepan over medium heat
  2. Add the onion, and cook until translucent and fragrant
  3. Meanwhile, mix the teriyaki sauce ingredients in a small bowl
  4. Add chicken to pan, and brown
  5. Add teriyaki sauce and lower the heat. Simmer for ~10 minutes or until meat is cooked and sauce is thickened.
  6. Optional: serve with rice and cut green onions.
