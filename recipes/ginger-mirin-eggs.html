Ginger Mirin Scrambled Eggs

Description

This delightful breakfast is slightly sweet and perfectly tangy thanks to the ginger powder.

If you want to make this into a full size meal, pair with steamed rice (white for flavor, brown for nutrition) and some pickled vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp mirin
  • 1 tbsp ginger powder
  • pinch of salt

Directions

  1. Heat a nonstick pan over a burner set to medium-high.
  2. Crack eggs into a bowl.
  3. Whisk the soy sauce, mirin, and ginger powder into the eggs until the yolks are thoroughly mixed.
  4. Gently pour seasoned egg mixture into the pan, and stir constantly. Be ready to plate the eggs as soon as they are cooked.
  5. (Optional) Serve with rice and pickles.
